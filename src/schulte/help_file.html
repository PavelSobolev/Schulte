What is Schulte table?

Knowledge of the world is not possible without attention. This is the method for the development of attention which is based on strict scientific basis. The method of "Searching symbols" or "Schulte Table", was developed as a psycho-diagnostic test to study the properties of attention by German psychiatrist and psychotherapist Walter Schulte (1910 - 1972).

How to use this method?

  1. Start training drill by clicking "Start new drill". After that first symbol appears in the central red cell and timer counting starts.
  2. Concentrate your eyes on the red cell and and try not to move them around..
  3. Without any movemenets of your eyes try to find the shown symbol in the table and then click corresponding cell by the mouse.
  4. Repeat last action again until the last symbol will be found and "thumb up" icon will appear.
  5. Check your time above the training field. The quicker is the better!

What does this training give?

Directing your attention to the playing field of the Schulte table, you control its concentration, depth and direction. Regular training can lead to increased clarity, visibility of content, speed of thinking and of finding information. This training can widen peripheral sight which is useful for quick reading.
